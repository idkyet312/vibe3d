===========================================
  VIBE3D ENGINE - VULKAN FORWARD+ RENDERER
===========================================

CONTROLS:
---------
WASD   - Move camera
Mouse  - Look around
TAB    - Toggle camera freeze (for GUI interaction)
Space  - Jump
Click  - Shoot
E      - Spawn objects
M      - Cycle materials
B      - Toggle shadow debug (Normal/Shadows/Cascades)
ESC    - Exit

MATERIAL CONTROLLER:
-------------------
Press TAB to freeze the camera and interact with the GUI.
- Choose from 10 material presets
- Adjust albedo color (RGB)
- Control roughness and metallic properties
- Set emissive glow (color and strength 0-100)
- Adjust lighting (skylight, sun intensity, sun direction)

SHADOW CONTROLS:
---------------
- Real-time shadow bias adjustment
- 4 cascaded shadow maps for optimal quality
- Debug modes to visualize shadows and cascades

PERFORMANCE:
-----------
- VSync disabled for maximum FPS
- 3 frames in flight
- 2048x2048 shadow maps
- 3x3 PCF filtering

GPU: NVIDIA GeForce RTX 4060
Architecture: Forward+ (Tiled Forward)
Expected FPS: 2000-2700 FPS

FEATURES:
--------
- Physically-based rendering (PBR)
- Cascaded shadow mapping
- Real-time emissive materials with floor glow
- ACES tonemapping for HDR
- ImGui embedded material controller
- Distance-based floor glow effect
